Privacy Policy

FMGE/NEET PG Study Scheduler respects your privacy. This application does not store any personal data on our servers. All study schedules you create remain on your device, and any Google Calendar integration is performed using secure OAuth tokens from Google.

We do not access, share, or sell your personal information. Your Google account and calendar data is only used to add events you specify to your calendar and is not stored or shared with any third party.

You can revoke the application's access to Google Calendar from your Google Account settings at any time.
